Who are we?

Cairo Hydraulics was founded in year 1970 by Eng. Abd El Malek Naguib, who was able to gain customer trust based on commitment to quality and timely delivered service.

The company ever since went through major developments and expansion after been managed by his son Eng. Hany.

Hany holds a B.Sc in Mechanical engineer science and he is a big believer that the “Customer” is by far the most important asset of the company
